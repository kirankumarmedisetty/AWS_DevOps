Q. Why DevOps?
Devops is needed because it bridges the gap between development and operations teams, leading to faster, more reliable software delivery and improved business outcomes. 

Q. Benefits of using DevOps?
Ans: *Enables continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD), reducing time to market and increase release frequency.
* Improves collabration between development, operations and other stakeholders, minimizing bottlenecks and miscommunication.
* Automates repetitive tasks, such as testing deployment and monitoring, resulting in fewer errors and greater efficiency. 
* Enhancing application quantity and stability with automatic feedback loops and real-time issue detection.
* Supports scalability and rapid adaption to changing bussiness requirements by making infrastructure management more agile.
